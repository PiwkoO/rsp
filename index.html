How to play?

Rock, scissors, paper is a hand game usually played shapes are rock, scissors and paper. A simultaneous, zero-sum game, it has only two possible outcomes: a draw, or a win for one player and a loss for other.
A player who decides to play rock will beat another player who has chosen scissors, but will lose to one who has played paper. A play of paper will lose to a play of scissors. If both players choose the same shape, the game is tied.

Select one of the symbols and confirm with the button . If you win the field will glow green and you will get a point. In case of loss, the field woll glow red and the computer will score a point. When box will glow yellow it means a draw and no one will get a point.
